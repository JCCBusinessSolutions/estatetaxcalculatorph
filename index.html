⚠️ CPA Certification RequiredGross Estate > ₱5M

Since the gross estate exceeds ₱5,000,000, BIR Form 1801 must be accompanied by a statement certified by a Philippine CPA, itemizing the assets, deductions, and computation above.

Filing Reminders

Deadline: File BIR Form 1801 within one (1) year from date of death.

Extension: Up to 5 years if settled judicially, or 2 years if settled extrajudicially.

Installments: Cash installments over 2 years permitted upon RDO approval, if payment in full would cause undue hardship.
